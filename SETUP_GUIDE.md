## ğŸ Understanding the Bar Configuration

![Hive Configuration Example](hive_configuration_example.png)

**How it works:**
1. Each bar in your Top Bar Hive is represented visually
2. Click any bar to cycle through status types
3. Colors show what's on each bar at a glance
4. Click "Capture" to save a snapshot
5. View history to track how your hive layout changes

**Why track bar configuration?**
- See your hive layout at a glance
- Track expansion/contraction patterns
- Plan interventions based on bar status
- Monitor brood nest movement
- Identify when to add/remove bars

## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this free?**  
A: Yes! TBH Beekeeper is free to use.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: Yes, once installed as a PWA, most features work offline. Data syncs when you reconnect.

**Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Sign in with the same account on any device.

**Q: What's the difference between bar configuration and inspections?**  
A: Bar configurations track the physical layout of your hive (which bars have brood, honey, etc.). Inspections record detailed observations like brood pattern quality, queen sightings, and temperament. They're separate but complementary features.
